PaperGraph can use a local HuggingFace sentence-transformer model to compute semantic similarity between papers.

  • Runs entirely on your machine — no data leaves your device
  • Requires Python 3.8+ on your system PATH
  • First-time setup downloads ~80–440 MB (model dependent)
  • You can change this later in ⚙ Similarity Settings
